List of Favorite Colors Class Activity

Short Summary of Activity

Activity to complement today's lesson on colors. Ask students to write their favorite colors on a slip of paper, then place the slips into a bowl and collect the bowl after all students put their slips in. Then create a table based on the student responses.The example table below shows how to present the data to the class.
